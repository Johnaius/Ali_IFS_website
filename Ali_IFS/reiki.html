Usui Reiki Master

"An Usui Reiki master can help someone by facilitating the flow of energy through their body, which is believed to promote physical, emotional, and spiritual healing. They may use various techniques such as hand placements and visualization to help balance and harmonize the energy within the body."

Usui Reiki is a form of energy healing that originated in Japan in the early 20th century. It was developed by Mikao Usui, a Buddhist monk who sought a way to promote physical, emotional, and spiritual healing. Usui believed that by connecting to a universal life force energy, individuals could heal themselves and others.


Usui Reiki is based on the principle that there is a universal life force energy that flows through all living things. This energy is said to be present in the body and can be accessed and harnessed to promote healing. Usui Reiki practitioners believe that when this energy is blocked or stagnant, it can lead to physical, emotional, and spiritual imbalances and illness. By removing blockages and facilitating the flow of energy, Usui Reiki can promote healing and wellness.


Usui Reiki is typically practiced through a series of hand placements on or near the body. The practitioner uses their hands to channel energy into the body, promoting relaxation and balance. Usui Reiki is a non-invasive practice, and the client remains fully clothed during the session. It is a gentle and safe healing modality that can be used alongside other medical or therapeutic treatments.


Usui Reiki has become increasingly popular in the Western world, and there are now many practitioners and teachers offering training and certification. Some practitioners specialize in specific areas, such as animal Reiki or Reiki for children. Usui Reiki can be a powerful tool for individuals seeking physical, emotional, or spiritual healing and can be used in a variety of settings, including hospitals, hospices, and wellness centers.
