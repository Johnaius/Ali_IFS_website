Hi I'm Ali

Welcome to my Website!

I am dedicated to helping my clients achieve physical, emotional, and spiritual balance through a variety of holistic practices.

As an Internal Family Systems (IFS) Level One Practitioner, I am also trained in a form of psychotherapy that recognizes the existence of multiple parts or subpersonalities within each individual. Through the IFS process, we can identify and work with these parts, helping to resolve their conflicts and create a sense of inner harmony, peace, and balance.

In addition to my IFS credentials, I am also a shamanic practitioner, trained in the use of traditional indigenous practices to facilitate healing and personal growth. These practices may include drumming, chanting, and journeying to connect with spirit guides and other non-ordinary sources of wisdom and guidance and exploration of non-ordinary or altered states of consciousness.

I am also a Usui Reiki Master, trained in the ancient art of energy healing. Reiki involves the transfer of energy through the practitioner to the client through the hands, with the intention of promoting relaxation, stress reduction, and overall well-being.

I offer a unique and personalized approach to healing that integrates these various practices and addresses the whole person. If you are seeking a path to greater peace, balance, and self-awareness, I encourage you to contact me to schedule a consultation. Together, we can work towards achieving your therapeutic goals and leading a happier, more fulfilling life.
