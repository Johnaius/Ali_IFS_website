Tantra

"Tantrika, or Tantra practitioners, can help someone by guiding them through the practice of Tantra, which involves integrating spiritual, sexual, and emotional energies. They may use various techniques, such as breathwork, meditation, and bodywork, to help individuals connect with their inner selves, release blockages, and promote healing and growth."

Tantric practice involves a wide range of techniques, including meditation, ritual, mantra recitation, yoga, and sexual practices. One of the key aspects of tantric practice is the use of energy, or prana, which is believed to flow through the body and can be directed and transformed through various techniques.


In tantric practice, sexual energy is seen as a powerful force that can be harnessed for spiritual transformation. Tantric sexual practices involve cultivating awareness and mindfulness during sexual activity, with the goal of achieving a state of profound union and connection with one's partner and with the divine.


In addition to sexual practices, tantric practice also involves the use of mantras, which are sacred sounds or phrases that are recited as a way of focusing the mind and connecting with divine energy. Tantra also emphasizes the importance of working with a qualified teacher or guru, who can provide guidance and support on the path to spiritual realization.


Tantric practice has been used for centuries as a way of promoting spiritual growth, enlightenment, and liberation. However, it's important to approach tantric practice with caution and to choose a qualified and experienced teacher or practitioner, as some aspects of tantric practice, particularly sexual practices, can be misused or misunderstood and may have negative effects if practiced improperly.
